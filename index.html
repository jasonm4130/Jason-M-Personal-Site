Jason Matthew

Webdesigner & Front end Developer

I'm a Brisbane, Australia based web designer and developer. I love challenges and opportunities to work and find soultions to problems for people. I have been working in the Web industry for the past 5 years starting with personal projects and moving from there into client work. For me the best part of being a designer and developer is helping people to bring their visions to life. Making a beautiful design seemingly appear out of a blank canvas.

In my spare time I enjoy fitness in general, from ParkRun on saturday mornings to gym on weekdays. I am always striving to improve myself as a person and in all the activities that I participate in. I am also a computer enthusiast and enjoy all things technology, including staying upto date with the latest tech news.

IOPA Website

A Website for a leading training organization.

Working closely with the Institute of Pacific Australia, we were able to design develop and deploy this website. Allowing the institute to pivot and provide their students with a wider range of opportunities.

We were able to work closely both on site together and offsite to allow for valuable input from the whole team resulting in the great design and an user experience for all who use the site.

  • Date: April 2017
  • Client: Iopa.com.au
  • Category: Web Design and Development

Lynk

An app aimed at promoting blood donation.

Lynk is an app design with an acompanying website aimed at promoting blood donating and saving lives. This app idea was created as part of a University course. A big part of this design was functionality and how the user would interact with the app and the website. Along with this major consideration the end goal of the app, to promote blood donation played a major role in the design.

  • Date: July 2015
  • Client: Lynk
  • Category: App and Web Design
